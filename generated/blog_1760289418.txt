### Unlocking Stories with Meaningful Questions  

Surface-level questions often lead to surface-level answers. To uncover deeper insights, focus on questions that explore emotions, values, and lessons:  

- **Reflections on childhood**: “What’s a memory from your childhood that still influences your decisions today?”  
- **Relationships and influences**: “Who shaped your life in a way they might not even realize?”  
- **Challenges and growth**: “What’s a moment when our family had to come together to overcome something difficult?”  
- **Values and principles**: “What decision are you most proud of, and what guided you in making it?”  
- **Treasured traditions**: “What little rituals made your family feel close and united?”
